About Me

Hello! I’m Deepanshu Rawat, a Computer Science and Engineering student with a strong passion for web development and problem-solving. Over the course of my journey, I’ve built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, which allows me to create responsive websites, design interactive user experiences, and write efficient, reusable code.

Beyond coding, I enjoy working with tools like Canva to design visuals, graphics, and presentations, which complement my technical work and allow me to express creativity. I primarily work in Visual Studio Code, which I consider my coding playground, where ideas turn into real projects.

I believe in continuous learning and enjoy exploring how technology and creativity can come together to build impactful solutions. My goal is to keep improving my skills, contribute to meaningful projects, and grow into a versatile developer who can take on real-world challenges with confidence.
